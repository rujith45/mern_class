Top 5 Kamal Haasan Movies

A tribute to the legendary Kamal Haasan.

Nayagan
Released: 1987
  • Crime, Drama
  • Loosely inspired by The Godfather
Cast: Kamal Haasan, Saranya
Director: Mani Ratnam
Producer: Muktha Srinivasan
Summary: The rise of an underworld don in Bombay who becomes a revered figure among the Tamil community while battling the burden of crime and fatherhood.
Indian
Released: 1996
  • Action, Thriller
  • Freedom fighter turned vigilante
Cast: Kamal Haasan, Manisha Koirala
Director: Shankar
Producer: A. M. Rathnam
Summary: An aged freedom fighter takes justice into his own hands to root out corruption, portraying dual roles and unforgettable makeup artistry.
Anbe Sivam
Released: 2003
  • Drama, Comedy
  • Journey of humanity and compassion
Cast: Kamal Haasan, Madhavan
Director: Sundar C.
Producer: P. L. Thenappan
Summary: A chance travel encounter between two contrasting individuals reveals life, philosophy, and love for mankind through the teachings of compassion (Sivam).
Vikram
Released: 2022
  • Action, Thriller
  • High-octane espionage
Cast: Kamal Haasan, Vijay Sethupathi, Fahadh Faasil
Director: Lokesh Kanagaraj
Producer: Raaj Kamal Films
Summary: A retired agent returns to action to dismantle a drug syndicate, showcasing a new era of Tamil action filmmaking and style.
Dasavathaaram
Released: 2008
  • Science Fiction, Drama
  • Kamal in 10 roles
Cast: Kamal Haasan, Asin
Director: K. S. Ravikumar
Producer: V. Ravichandran
Summary: A bio‑weapon conspiracy unravels across centuries as ten avatars of Kamal Haasan unfold in a genre‑bending performance.
